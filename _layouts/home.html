ABOUT ME

I love learning new software that challenges my critical thinking and enhancing skills I have been working on for years. I am excited to branch out with my skills and continuously grow as a professional.

When I am not exploring new trails with my wife, Jill, and our golden retriever, Quasky, I also enjoy watching and playing sports. I also love playing golf, disc golf, and any type of pick-up sport that calls for getting outside.

Sports have been a centralized part of my life, and fueled the competitive edge that I have brought to my professional working career.
